Bench Sponsorship

Sponsored memorial and commemorative benches are one of the most emotionally resonant revenue streams available to private estates. A bench in a beautiful place, bearing the name of someone loved, is a gift that lasts for decades.

The model is straightforward. Visitors browse available locations on a map of your estate, choose somewhere that means something to them, and select a price. We handle everything from that point — booking confirmation, bench procurement, installation, and the engraved plaque. Annual renewal payments, as families renew their sponsorship each year, add a reliable recurring element to the income stream.

Getting the balance right matters. We work with you to identify a thoughtful number of carefully chosen locations — enough to generate meaningful income, but never so many as to clutter a beautiful view or detract from the landscape.

What we handle

Location selection

We advise on the right number and placement of locations for your estate.

Online booking

A seamless booking flow on your estate page handles payments and confirmation.

Procurement & installation

We coordinate bench selection, engraving, delivery, and installation.

Annual renewals

Recurring annual income as sponsors renew. We manage the renewal communications.
