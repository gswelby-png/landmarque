Legacies

For estates with deep ties to their communities and strong landscape appeal, legacies can be among the most significant long-term revenue streams of all. Many visitors to beautiful private land would choose to leave a gift to it in their will — they simply do not know it is possible, or how to go about it.

LandMarque makes the process simple, dignified, and easy to discover. We provide sensitive, well-crafted copy for your estate page that explains the option clearly and without any pressure. Visitors who are moved to leave a legacy can begin the process in a few clicks.

Unlike parking or bench sponsorship, legacies compound over time. An estate that begins promoting legacy giving today is building a revenue stream that may not materialise immediately, but that can prove transformative in the years and decades ahead. We handle all the copy and the process — you simply review and approve the messaging.

Why legacies work

  • — People who love your land are natural legacy givers — they simply need to be told it is possible
  • — No upfront cost or ongoing effort once the page is live
  • — Income compounds over time as awareness grows among regular visitors
  • — Entirely voluntary and sensitivity-led — never pushy or transactional
