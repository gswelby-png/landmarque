Parking

For most estates offering public access, parking is the single fastest and most significant new revenue stream available. Visitors are already arriving — the opportunity is simply to capture a fair contribution from those visits.

Our approach is entirely light-touch and voluntary. Visitors are invited to pay via a QR code at the car park entrance, which takes them to a payment page branded entirely to your estate — your logo, your colours, your welcome message. There are no barriers, no fines, no enforcement. The messaging is warm and honest: that their contribution helps maintain the facilities they are enjoying.

For car parks that are already long established, there are typically no additional planning restrictions or legal requirements. Our onboarding process takes a matter of minutes, and with signage in place, most estates are operational within a week.

How it works

01
