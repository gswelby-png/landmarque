Tree Sponsorship

Tree sponsorship combines a growing appetite for environmental action with the deep human instinct to leave something living behind. Whether a single oak planted in memory of someone loved, or a programme of woodland expansion, trees offer a revenue stream that genuinely enhances your landscape.

We help you create and promote a tree sponsorship scheme that feels authentic to your land. Sponsors choose from available locations on a map, select a species where appropriate, and receive a certificate of sponsorship. A modest annual contribution towards maintenance keeps the relationship — and the income — ongoing.

Corporate sponsors are an increasingly important part of this market. Businesses seeking credible, locally meaningful carbon offset or community engagement are natural partners for estates with planting programmes. We handle the booking flow, the certificates, the communications, and coordination with your land management team.
