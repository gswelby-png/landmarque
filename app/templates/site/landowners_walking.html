Walking Routes

Walking is the primary reason most people visit private estates. Well-curated, clearly presented routes turn a casual visit into a memorable experience — and give people a reason to return, to bring friends, and to pay for parking.

We help you define, describe, and publish walking routes from your estate. Each route appears on your estate page with distance, estimated duration, difficulty, and a description of the highlights — accessible on any device without downloading anything. Routes update easily as your access arrangements change with the seasons.

Well-defined routes also serve a practical purpose: by channelling walkers onto established paths, they can help reduce erosion, keep visitors away from sensitive areas, and make it easier to manage access responsibly across your land.

Each route includes

  • — Distance, duration, and difficulty rating
  • — A curated description of highlights along the way
  • — Key points of interest and seasonal notes
  • — Easy to update seasonally through your estate dashboard
